1. **Scientific Elegance**
   - Clean, professional appearance
   - Subtle animations inspired by microscopy
   - Cells and biological themes

   - Important info stands out (red accents)
   - Secondary info is muted (grey)
   - Tertiary info is subtle (light grey)

   - High contrast text
   - Large click targets (48px minimum)
   - Clear focus indicators
   - Semantic HTML

4. **Consistency**
   - Same spacing throughout (8px grid)
   - Consistent colors (CryoCord palette)
   - Unified typography (Inter font)
   - Matching border radius (8-12px)
